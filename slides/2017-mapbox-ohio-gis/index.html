What

Are Vector Tiles?

Vector Tiles are a representation of geographic features and their underlying data for a specific zoom level and bounding box, typically served as .pbf files. Lines and polygons may be simplified, and points may be omitted. - Adapted from Wikipedia

Mapbox Vector Tiles (MVT)

MVT is slowly becoming an industry standard.
